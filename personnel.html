CLASSIFICATION LEVEL: LEVEL 4 CLEARANCE REQUIRED

Security Clearance Levels

Level 0

Janitors, maintenance workers, and other support staff who do not require access to sensitive information.

Access Permissions:
  • Basic facility areas
  • Non-sensitive maintenance zones
  • Staff cafeteria and amenities

Level 1

Technical staff, security guards, and research assistants with limited access to SCPs.

Access Permissions:
  • Safe-class containment zones
  • Basic research facilities
  • Security checkpoints

Level 2

Researchers, senior security personnel, and containment specialists.

Access Permissions:
  • Euclid-class research zones
  • Medium security areas
  • Research laboratories

Level 3

Senior researchers, project heads, and site security chiefs.

Access Permissions:
  • Keter-class containment zones
  • High security areas
  • Classified research data

Level 4

Site directors, department heads, and senior administration.

Access Permissions:
  • All containment zones
  • Administrative access
  • Personnel files

Level 5

O5 Council members and designated personnel only.
