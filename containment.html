Containment Procedures and Protocols

CLASSIFICATION LEVEL: LEVEL 3 CLEARANCE REQUIRED

Standard Containment Protocols

  • All personnel must maintain Level 2 clearance or higher
  • Biometric scanning required for facility access
  • Regular psychological evaluations mandatory
  • Emergency procedures must be memorized
  • No unauthorized experiments or testing

Hazard Classifications

  • Safe - Easily contained, minimal risk
  • Euclid - Unpredictable, requires monitoring
  • Keter - Highly dangerous, difficult to contain
  • Thaumiel - Used for containment
  • Apollyon - Uncontainable, world-ending threat

Facility Requirements

  • Reinforced steel containment cells
  • Electromagnetic shielding
  • Backup power systems
  • Multiple security checkpoints
  • Advanced surveillance systems

Personnel Protocol

  • Regular rotation of security teams
  • Mandatory protective equipment
  • Communication protocols strictly enforced
  • Emergency response training required
  • Class-A amnestics available if needed

Emergency Procedures

  • Site-wide lockdown protocols
  • Containment breach responses
  • Evacuation procedures
  • Decontamination protocols
  • Communications blackout procedures

Research Guidelines

  • Testing approval required
  • Documentation mandatory
  • Cross-testing prohibited
  • Sample handling procedures
  • Results classification protocol

NOTICE: Violation of containment procedures may result in immediate termination and/or administration of amnestics.
